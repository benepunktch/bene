About the Brand

Bené is a young and growing brand, founded in the heart of Switzerland as part of a high school graduation project. Today, it continues to evolve – driven by curiosity, passion, and a love for creative expression.

Bené represents a fusion of streetwear and luxury. This project is not about chasing profit – it’s about learning valuable life skills, developing creativity, and exploring what’s possible when passion meets commitment.

From web design and coding to creating professional graphics and clothing concepts – everything is developed by myself. I want to improve over time and embrace every challenge that comes with building something from scratch.

This platform is for friends, family, and anyone around the world. Every supporter doesn't just help the brand to grow – they help me to grow personally.

You don't make me rich in money – but you make me rich in knowledge.

Thanks for being part of the journey. This is just the beginning.
